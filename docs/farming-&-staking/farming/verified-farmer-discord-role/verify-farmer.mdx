We are thrilled to announce the **Verified Farmer** role on our [Discord server](https://discord.com/invite/subspace-network)!
You can track your farmer through the [Astral Block Explorer.](https://explorer.subspace.network/gemini-3h/consensus#/gemini-3h/consensus)

**Eligibility for the Verified Farmer Role**

**If you have earned a block or vote reward**, you can now link your Discord account to your farmer wallet through the Astral application to obtain this role. Hereâ€™s how to do it:

1. Navigate to the wallet panel by clicking on the top right corner of the application.
![wallet panel](/img/doc-imgs/verify-farmer/verify-farmer.png)
2. Find the new "Connect Discord" section within the panel.
3. Press the button to start the OAuth authorization process.
4. Complete the authorization by following the on-screen instructions.
5. Once the process is successful, the **Verified Farmer** role will be automatically assigned to you on our Discord server.


Should you have any questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to the team on Discord!
